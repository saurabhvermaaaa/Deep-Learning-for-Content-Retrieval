India won 11 medals at US Open Karate Championship

India has won a total of 11 medals which is including three gold, two silver, and then six bronze, at the recently concluded event of US Open Karate Championship in the place of Las Vegas. Sensei Yashpal Singh Kalsi,  Harcharan Singh Chowhan, Shaifali Agarwal, Abhishek Sengupta, and Rantej Singh were been part of the five-member of the Indian team. The prestigious annual Karate event which saws the participation of 2,374 number of players from 42 countries.
